Our Models

Diabetes Prediction:

Our Diabetes Prediction model is meticulously crafted to provide accurate insights into the likelihood of diabetes onset. Leveraging cutting-edge techniques such as missing value handling, outlier detection and handling, and extensive exploratory data analysis (EDA), we've engineered a robust solution. Through rigorous model training and hyperparameter tuning, we've honed the predictive power of our algorithm. Selecting the best model architecture, we've achieved an impressive accuracy of 94.5%. With our model, individuals can proactively assess their risk of diabetes and take proactive steps towards prevention and management.

Heart Disease:

Our Heart Disease Prediction model is designed to empower individuals with insights into their cardiovascular health. Employing advanced methodologies including missing value handling, outlier detection and handling, and thorough exploratory data analysis (EDA), we've developed a reliable tool. Through rigorous model training and hyperparameter tuning, we've optimized our algorithm for accuracy. Our efforts have resulted in a commendable accuracy rate of 88.52%. With our model, users can assess their risk of heart disease and make informed decisions to prioritize their cardiac well-being.

Stroke Prediction:

With our Stroke Prediction model, we offer a sophisticated approach to assess the risk of stroke occurrence. Utilizing a comprehensive range of techniques such as missing value handling, outlier detection and handling, and in-depth exploratory data analysis (EDA), we've crafted a robust solution. Through meticulous model training and hyperparameter tuning, we've fine-tuned our algorithm for optimal performance. As a result, our model boasts an impressive accuracy rate of 96.2%. By leveraging our model, individuals can gain valuable insights into their stroke risk and take proactive measures to safeguard their health.
